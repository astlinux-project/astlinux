AstLinux Wiki

AstLinux Wiki Highlights:

  • Replace /mnt/kd/wiki/index.html (or index.php) with your custom wiki web page.
  • Only readable by 'staff' or 'admin' web users.
  • Optional "Wiki" link, at upper-right, use Prefs tab → General Options:
       External Wiki Link: https://localhost/admin/wiki/
  • Optional "Wiki" tab, use Prefs tab → General Options: check "Show Wiki Tab"
